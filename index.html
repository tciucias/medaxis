Medaxis

NICE guideline quick-reference cards for GPs and GP trainees. Built to support confident, evidence-based consultations.

About

Medaxis is an independent clinical reference tool developed for use in UK primary care. Content is derived from NICE guidelines and CKS and is intended for educational purposes only. It is not independently verified by NICE and does not replace clinical judgement.
